Spatial Root

Universal ADM transcoder and live rendering engine. Play Atmos, 360RA, or other ADMs back on any speaker layout. Format transcoding supported.

Designed for researchers, spatial audio engineers, and composers working at the intersection of acoustics and perception. Built in C++ with a clean DSP-first architecture.
