## Company overview

CULT DSP builds open tools for spatial audio rendering, playback, and metadata workflows. The goal is to make immersive formats usable in realtime and interactive contexts, including NGA and spatial computing. We design for research labs, artists, and immersive venues with flexible routing, layout-agnostic playback, and clear metadata pipelines.

## What we offer

- Spatial rendering and realtime playback to arbitrary speaker layouts
- Interactive spatial mix solutions (control, routing, scene-aware playback)
- Immersive consulting (system design, layout strategy, pipeline planning)
- Metadata transcoding for NGA and spatial computing workflows

## Spatial Root

Spatial Root is an ADM transcoding and playback engine for immersive audio. It focuses on realtime, layout-agnostic rendering so Dolby Atmos / ADM projects and other object-based sources can play to any speaker configuration with routing and live control.

Key idea: bring DAW mixes into realtime contexts such as installations, research setups, performance systems, and spatial computing prototypes.

## Spatial Seed

Spatial Seed is a procedural spatial upmixer that outputs ADM. It uses machine learning to let users navigate a latent space matrix to shape a mix. The generated ADMs can be:

- Played back in Spatial Root
- Opened in a DAW for review, refinement, and delivery

Key idea: fast generation that stays in editable ADM metadata rather than a closed pipeline.
