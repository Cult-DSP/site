Company overview:
CULT DSP builds open, modern tools for spatial audio rendering, playback, and metadata workflows—bridging today’s immersive formats with the needs of next-gen interactive media (NGA + spatial computing). We design for research labs, artists, and immersive venues: flexible routing, layout-agnostic playback, and transparent metadata pipelines.


What we offer:
- Spatial rendering + realtime playback to arbitrary speaker layouts
- Interactive spatial mix solutions (control, routing, scene-aware playback)

- Immersive consulting (system design, layout strategy, pipeline planning)

- Metadata transcoding for the future of NGA and spatial computing


on spatial root:
Spatial Root is an ADM transcoding + playback engine for immersive audio. It focuses on realtime, layout-agnostic rendering—enabling Dolby Atmos / ADM workflows (and other object-based sources) to be played to any speaker configuration with robust routing and live control.

Key idea: bring “DAW-grade” immersive mixes into realtime environments—installations, research, performance, and spatial computing prototypes.





on spatial seed:
Spatial Seed is a procedural spatial upmixer that outputs ADM. These generated ADMs can be:

played back directly in Spatial Root, or

opened in a DAW for review, refinement, and final delivery

Key idea: fast, controllable immersive generation that stays inside open, editable metadata (ADM) instead of a locked pipeline.
