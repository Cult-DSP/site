About CULT DSP

Instruments
built with
discipline.

CULT DSP is an independent research and development project focused on digital signal processing tools for spatial audio, acoustic modelling, and generative sound design.

The work sits at the intersection of mathematics, acoustics, and musical practice. Every tool is built to be correct first — then usable, then beautiful.

The Mission

Too many DSP tools obscure their inner workings behind opaque interfaces. CULT DSP builds instruments you can understand — and extend.

We believe that rigorous signal theory and poetic sound design are not at odds. The goal is tools that are genuinely useful to musicians, researchers, and builders alike.
